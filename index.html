About Me

Caleb is a stand-up comic, writer, and producer.

As a stand-up, he's been lucky enough to feature for THE HILARIOUS Jenny Zigrino (featured on Dropout.TV, Sirius XM) and opened for the gorgeous and wildly-witty BBC shortlisted comic, Lady Buhsra.

He co-created, co-stars and is currently executive producing Luigi the Musical, a viral dark comedy and scaled the show to over 3,000 tickets sold, to Edinburg Fringe and international press coverage.

He's lived in 16 states, but mostly grew up in the South, where he was too scared to share his dreams of performing. Now he's making up for lost time, joining the comedy scene as a finalist in the NYC Queer Comedy Festival, a featured comic in LGBTLol Fest, and performing at venues like Cobb's Comedy Club, The Stand, Gotham Comedy Club, ColdTowne Theater and venues across Edinburgh Fringe.
