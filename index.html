About Me

I'm a stand-up comedian who's been making audiences laugh across the country. With a unique perspective and relatable humor, I bring fresh energy to every stage.

My comedy has been featured on major platforms, and I've performed at venues ranging from intimate clubs to large festivals. Whether it's observational humor, storytelling, or sharp wit, I bring my A-game to every show.

When I'm not on stage, you can find me creating content for Instagram and TikTok, where I share daily laughs and behind-the-scenes moments from my comedy journey.
